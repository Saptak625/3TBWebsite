Our Mission

WE AIM TO DESTIGMATIZE HEARING LOSS AND CREATE A MORE INCLUSIVE SOCIETY THROUGH OUR “HEAR” PRINCIPLES:

Help

the underprivileged hearing loss community by providing essential equipment and materials.

Learn how we are fulfilling the Help Pillar!
Educate

youth about different types of hearing loss and how to protect their hearing by practicing healthy hearing habits.

Learn how we are fulfilling the Educate Pillar!
Advocate

for youth with hearing loss to raise awareness and have safer hearing experiences as a society.

Learn how we are fulfilling the Advocate Pillar!
Reach

kids and teens with hearing loss by creating a community to support each other through their challenges.

Learn how we are fulfilling the Reach Pillar!
